A sealion is a type of marine mammal that belongs to the family Otariidae. They are characterized by their ability to walk on all four flippers on land and their external ear flaps. Sealions are found in many parts of the world, including the Pacific and Atlantic Oceans. They are carnivorous animals that feed mostly on fish and squid.
