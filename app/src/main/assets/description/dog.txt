A dog is a mammal and a domesticated carnivorous animal that belongs to the Canidae family. They are one of the most popular pets worldwide and have been bred by humans for thousands of years for various purposes, including hunting, herding, guarding, and companionship. Dogs come in different breeds, shapes, sizes, and colors, with over 300 recognized breeds worldwide. They have a keen sense of smell, excellent hearing, and are known for their loyalty and affection towards their owners. Dogs require socialization, exercise, and proper training to ensure they become well-behaved and healthy pets.
