Crab is a type of seafood that come from a group of crustaceans found in the ocean. There are many different species of crab, each with their own unique characteristics and flavor profiles. Some common species of crab include blue crab, Dungeness crab, king crab, snow crab, and stone crab. Crab meat is considered a delicacy in many parts of the world and is often used in a variety of dishes, such as crab cakes, crab soup, and crab legs with butter.
